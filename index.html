Vision


Advanced Machine Learning (AML) lab aims to play a crucial role in taking the BUBT towards research-based education in global standards. We aspire to exhibit intelligent systems for solving real-world complexities. We are deploying Deep Learning algorithms, which are a new domain of Machine Learning that moves towards the dream goals of modern invention. The core aspirations of the lab are as follows:

  • To focus on developing world-class applied research resources in Machine learning.
  • To collaboratively interpret and deploy critical solutions engaging Computer Science, Robotics, Medical Science, Bioinformatics, Social Science, Life Science, and so on.
  • To engage capable and talented researchers and provide them with an environment and direction for creating innovative technologies and products.
  • To bridge the existing gap between academia and industry by training the young graduates according to the requirements of the job market through active collaboration with the international lab and local industry.
  • To nurture and promote the culture of result-oriented research and development activities and using artificial intelligence for the socio-economic development of our country.

Mission

AML aspires to become pioneer research and advanced technology organization in the fields of Machine Learning (ML) and Information Technology and establish itself as a centre of excellence recognized for cutting-edge research, technology innovation, and industrial collaboration to become the leading R&D center committed to the advancement of science and technology in Bangladesh.
